Intro...

A triangle is a polygon with three edges and three vertices. It is one of the basic shapes in geometry. Here, I'll talk about three types;

#1: Equilateral triangle.

This is a triangle in which all three sides are equal.

#2: Isosceles triangle.

This is a triangle that has two sides of equal length.

#3: Scalene triangle.

This is a triangle that has three unequal sides

Finally...

It's good to note that I've assumed that a triangle is "Not A Triangle" if the sum of any two sides of the triangle is equal to or less than the third remaining side.
